What is causKnow after all?

1. causKnow is a semantic web-based application deployed on a cloud architecture.

2. Basically, causKnow lays its foundation over ontological engineering principles. It makes use of a multi-layered ontological formalism for context-aware dissemination of causal knowledge about a given phenomenon. Read this scientific publication to find more. (https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1474034618301642)

3. At its core, causKnow appreciates certain epistemological concerns around knowledge sharing and reuse; by adding a context-sensing power to its knowledge retrieval mechanism. End user can define contextual attributes of interest and query a graph-based database containing FCM-driven causal knowledge representations.

4. The current meta-model of causKnow’s knowledge representation and reasoning scheme supports the Fuzzy Cognitive Mapping approach. FCM, combining the principles of neuro-computing and Fuzzy logic, is an intuitive tool which exploits expert causal judgements for anticipation of future states of highly dynamic, distributed, and complex systems.

5. At the moment, causKnow allows for uploading FCM scenarios as ontological instances in various serializations such as RDF/XML, Turtle, N3, etc.

6. We believe in open knowledge-sharing in action! Find causKnow’s Github repo here: Falconio
